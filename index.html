About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a premier industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to advancing excellence in digital risk, cyber risk, and AI governance. Positioned at the intersection of technology and regulation, we partner closely with leading UK universities to deliver rigorous, real-world education tailored to the needs of regulated industries and critical infrastructure sectors.

Our unique model bridges the gap between academic theory and practical application. By leading real-world project deployments and focusing on emerging threats, we ensure our professionals are uniquely equipped to navigate complex risk frameworks, build operational resilience, and drive secure innovation in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape.
